Inspection Equipment

Mascot CNC is a distributor of the best quality Inspection Equipment. Compliance and quality are everything in the manufacturing industry. We are the distributor of Inspection Equipment from the most trusted manufacturers including Luthra Precision Equipment, Armatech, and Baker Gauges.

We provide a range of inspection equipment of the highest precision and quality. We keep in mind the specific functionality and operation of your company and suggest to you the inspection equipment that is just right for your needs.

The range of Inspection Equipment includes Surface Plate, Comparator Stand, Bench Center, Angle Plate, V Block, Dial Magnetic Stand, and Portable Surface Roughness Tester.
