OUR SCHOOL HISTORY

From The Management's Desk

With the Divine Blessings of Lord Anjaneya, Green Park Educational Institutions were established in 2007 at Postal Nagar, Bodhupatty. With a unique record of creating innumerable Medicos and Engineers for more than a decade, Green Park is a stand-out institution in the state. Dedicated itself to propelling the students to excellence in Education along with "All Round Development’, Team Green Park relentlessly works on integrated education with innovative use of science and technology to develop world class citizens, through a holistic approach.
With a team of well - qualified, extensively trained, highly passionate and strongly committed faculty, the Institution constantly encourages its students to explore beyond the curriculum.
The Management regularly monitors the academic progress of all the students. Special remedial programmes are conducted for late-bloomers. Our Trust is so magnanimous that it offers free education to 200 meritorious students every year for those who excel in Talent Search Examination conducted by the Trust. Apart from this, our Trust liberally contributes for various charitable activities for the welfare of our society and thus humanity.
With unflinching fervour and unabated passion, we run Green Park International Senior Secondary School which caters to the needs of students dreaming to learn through CBSE stream.
The Almighty’s will, your unconditional and all time support have made our institutions what we are today. Our march in pursuit of glory and our mission to excel further in all fronts will continue with your unstinted blessings and support.
