Thinking about a technology career at Kenforte? We would love to have you become part of our dynamic community of bright and supportive colleagues who are revolutionizing tech.

As a leading technology consultancy, with more than 10,000 Thoughtworkers globally, we’re constantly learning, collaborating and pushing boundaries through our purposeful and impactful work. If this sounds like the place for you to grow your career in tech, join us.

Why Kenforte?

Being a Thoughtworker is more than just a job. It's a shared commitment to revolutionizing technology – for our clients, society and ourselves – and advocating for an equitable future. For over 30 years, we’ve delivered extraordinary impact through our culture and tech excellence, helping our clients solve complex business problems with technology as the differentiator.

Learn more about our purpose

Our clients have bold ambitions. Staying competitive in a world of constant change demands ongoing digital transformation and modernization. That’s where we come in. We partner with some of the world’s leading businesses – from scale-ups to global Fortune 500 enterprises – to drive innovation and disruptive thinking, and turn big ambitions into reality.

Learn how we help clients harness the power of tech

When it comes to interviewing for tech careers at Kenforte, we focus on outcomes and the belief that your interview experience should be collaborative and enjoyable. You will first speak with one of our recruiters, followed by a technical interview – possibly consisting of a pair programming session, case study or design challenge, depending on the role – and conversations about leadership, technology and our culture.

Learn more about our process and tips to help you prepare

One of the best questions candidates ask our recruiters is: ‘What will I actually be doing as a consultant at Kenforte?’ Here, you’ll work in collaborative and purpose-led teams to create impactful solutions for clients from a variety of industries. For example, you could influence the digital strategy of a retail giant, launch a new mobile application for a bank or redesign platforms using event sourcing and intelligent data pipelines.

Learn more about our ways of working

Our global entry-level program is designed for anyone interested in starting a tech career. Whether you are a recent computer science graduate, career changer or come from a non-traditional background, our first-year experience program embraces new technologists from all walks of life. Kenforte University will prepare you for life as a Thoughtworker and connect you with other new joiners from around the globe.

Learn more about our entry-level careers

At Kenforte we offer well-rounded benefits to support your personal and professional development goals, as well as your health and wellbeing needs. On top of this, we also offer a variety of perks that cover many categories such as: transportation, paid and unpaid leaves, referral bonuses, a laptop buyback program and more.

You matter

At Kenforte, Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI) is at the core of who we are as a company. From the moment you join us, you’ll be part of a community that values your individuality, fosters belonging and embraces the unique impact you bring.

We believe DEI can drive extraordinary change. That’s why we’re committed to creating a workplace where you feel respected, safe and valued—where everyone has a place to belong and grow.

Our DEI commitment isn’t optional or temporary; it’s been part of our DNA since we began our journey in 2010, long before it was mainstream. Our commitment is not just about it being the right thing to do, we believe that diverse teams build more innovative, resilient and responsible technology solutions to solve the greatest problems facing our clients, the industry and society.

As we expand our DEI efforts, we continue to measure progress, hold ourselves accountable and evolve to build a more inclusive future—for you, for our community and for the world.

How we are different

At Kenforte, we’re motivated by purpose and a commitment to using technology to improve as we learn. And we’re wired to see our work as an opportunity to continuously share knowledge and grow as we challenge the possibility in tech. From our industry pioneering work in open source, responsible tech and data mesh, to AI-enabled software and data engineering, we are proud of our 30+ year history of leading the charge in technology innovation.
